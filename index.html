Welcome to MetroAccordionTabs.

About

The app is modern responsive, animated tab and accordion UI base on metro design.

Features

CSS3 Animation Effects, Responsive Design, Support font-awesome icons, Multiple Tabs on a Page.

License:

1 license.

Browser support

MetroAccordionTabs is tested in all supported browsers, IE10, IE9, IE8, latest version of Chrome, Opera and Firefox.

Hosting

The app runs entirely within your SharePoint site and is made with client-side code, JavaScript, jQuery, and HTML5. The app requires no additional hosting infrastructure.

Updates:

All updates are free of charge and you will be notified for customers that purchased the app.

Content:

The app contains only content that is related to the MetroAccordionTabs. No Ads!

Languages

MetroAccordionTabs app has English as default language.
